CalmGuard- Your Stress and Strain Sentinal

Our team of students has come up with an innovative project that taps into the power of machine learning and computer vision. Through a webcam, our system can track stress levels and monitor eye strain in real time. The main aim is to tackle the downsides of extended computer use by providing users with personalized insights to handle stress better and reduce eye strain.
